How it all started

Delivering Strategic Value Instead of Mindless Development

WebsiteHandy was established in 2014 with the vision to address a critical gap in the digital landscape - the need for comprehensive, top-quality website maintenance, design, and development. Our founder, a skilled web developer, recognized that these three elements should not stand alone, but instead form a cohesive, integrated solution. Thus, the foundation for WebsiteHandy was laid.

We are more than just a website maintenance company; we are a full-service digital partner. Our team of seasoned designers, developers, and marketing professionals collaborate closely, ensuring that every website we manage is not only aesthetically pleasing and functionally robust but also primed for ease of maintenance and updates.

Our steadfast commitment to excellence and our holistic approach have positioned us as a go-to provider for businesses in need of reliable and effective website solutions. We've earned our clients' trust by consistently keeping their websites in optimal shape, which frees them to concentrate on their core operations.

WebsiteHandy prides itself on its in-depth understanding of web technologies, its capacity to deliver cost- effective, high-quality solutions, and its unwavering focus on user-friendly interfaces. As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our mission to be a leading provider of integrated website maintenance, design, and development services
